Non-Vegetarian Biryani

Description

Spicy, aromatic rice dish cooked with chicken and Indian spices. A festive favorite!

Ingredients:

  • Basmati rice
  • Chicken pieces
  • Onions
  • Yogurt
  • Biryani Spices (garam massala, turmeric, etc)
  • Ginger-garlic paste

Steps:

  1. Cook chicken with spices and yogurt.
  2. Half cook rice separately.
  3. Layer rice and chicken, cook together.
  4. Garnish with fried onions and lemon.
