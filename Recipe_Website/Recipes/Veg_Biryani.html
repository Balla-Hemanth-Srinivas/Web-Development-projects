Vegetarian Biryani

Description

A fragrant rice dish cooked with mixed vegetables and spices. Perfect for vegetarians.

Ingredients:

  • Basmati rice
  • Mixed vegetables (carrot, beans, peas)
  • Biryani Masala
  • Onions
  • Yogurt

Steps:

  1. Cook vegetables with spices and yogurt.
  2. Half cook rice separately.
  3. Layer rice and veggies, cook together.
  4. Serve hot with raita.
