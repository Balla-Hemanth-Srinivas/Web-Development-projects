Rainbow Fruit Salads

Description

A colorful mix of fresh fruits tossed together for a healthy and refreshing snack.

Ingredients:

  • Strawberries
  • Kiwi
  • Blueberries
  • Raspberries
  • Apple

Steps:

  1. Chop all fruits into bite-sized pieces.
  2. Mix them gently in a bowl.
  3. Serve fresh or chill for later.
