Any Starter

Description

Spicy and juicy fried chicken strips, famous in Andhra-style starters.

Ingredients:

  • Chicken stripes
  • Yogurt
  • Chilli powder
  • Curry leaves
  • Cornflour

Steps:

  1. Marinate chicken with spices and yogurt.
  2. Deep fry till crispy.
  3. Toss with curry leaves and serve hot.
