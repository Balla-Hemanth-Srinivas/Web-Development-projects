Deserts

Description

A creamy frozen dessert topped with sweet cherries and crunchy nuts. Easy, no-bake, and perfect for summer.

Ingredients

  • Whipped cream
  • Egg yolks
  • Sugar
  • Vanilla
  • Cherries
  • Nuts

Steps

  1. Cook cherries with sugar to make sauce.
  2. Mix whipped cream, egg yolks, sugar, vanilla, and nuts.
  3. Pour into a pan, freeze.
  4. Top with cherry sauce and nuts before serving.
